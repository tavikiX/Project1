About us

We can do car service, we lend cars at a fee, or have them bought with insurance and also allow our customers to sell to us their used cars.

We have been in this line of work since 1975 and our profits have improved over the years and in this present decade, by a big margin.

We are grateful to our supporters, our stakeholders and shareholders, our customers, our employees and the board of management for the continued moral support and the cash inflow.

With the community in my control and trust, we pledge to always make the customers happy and satisfied with our products and services and provide help/assistance to the troubles of our system.
